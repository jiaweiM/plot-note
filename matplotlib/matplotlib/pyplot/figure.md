# matplotlib.pyplot.figure

***

```python
matplotlib.pyplot.figure(
    num=None, figsize=None, dpi=None, *, facecolor=None, edgecolor=None, frameon=True, FigureClass=<class 'matplotlib.figure.Figure'>, clear=False, **kwargs)
```

## 参数
